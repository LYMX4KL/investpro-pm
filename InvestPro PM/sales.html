Selling Process

What to expect when you list with InvestPro

A clear, step-by-step path from first conversation to closing day.

01

Free Valuation

We pull recent comps, walk the property, and prepare a comparative market analysis (CMA) with a realistic listing range — no obligation.

02

Prep & Photos

Light staging consult, professional photography (often drone too), 3D virtual tour, and a tightly written MLS narrative. Listing typically goes live in under a week.

03

Showings & Offers

Lockbox + appointments coordinated for you. Weekly showing reports. We negotiate offers with a clear strategy — not just the highest number, but the strongest terms.

04

Close & Hand Off

Inspection coordination, escrow, repair negotiations, and final walkthrough. We're at the table on closing day — not just emailing.
