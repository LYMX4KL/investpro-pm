About InvestPro Realty

A Las Vegas brokerage built around a simple belief: when the brokerage takes care of its people, everything else takes care of itself.

Our Mission

Empower agents. Build wealth. Share success.

We empower real estate professionals, create lasting wealth, and build a thriving community of success. Every agent deserves the financial benefits of brokerage ownership — without the burdens of overhead, liability, or isolation.

Our Vision

Redefine how real estate professionals grow.

Through collaboration, shared rewards, and lifelong partnerships. Our compensation structure, our owner relationships, and our agent program are all designed for decades — not quick wins.

Our Story

Built by an agent. For agents — and their clients.

InvestPro Realty was founded by Kenny Lin, a 14-year Las Vegas real estate veteran who has personally walked the road from new agent to small-brokerage owner. He knows the struggle of balancing transactions, agent retention, marketing, and growth — and he built InvestPro to fix what most brokerages get wrong.

We do three things, and we do them well: residential sales, professional property management, and an agent program that pays a real residual income for the work you do today.

Our office in central Las Vegas is the home base for owners across the valley, tenants in our managed portfolio, and a growing team of agents who chose InvestPro because it actually invests in them.
