What happens next

  1. ✓
    Application received

    Submitted just now. You'll receive an email confirmation at the address you provided.

  2. ✓
    Application + Holding Fee paid

    Stripe receipts sent to your email. Application fee is non-refundable; Holding Fee will be refunded if denied or applied to your deposit if approved.

  3. 2
    Admin review & screening

    Our admin team verifies your documents, runs credit + background checks, and (if applicable) sends you a PetScreening.com link. Usually 1–2 business days.

  4. 3
    PM / Owner approval

    The property manager (and owner, when required) reviews the screening summary and decides.

  5. 4
    Term sheet (if approved)

    You'll receive a term sheet by email with the proposed lease terms — accept or decline.

  6. 5
    Lease signing & orientation

    Schedule a date to sign the lease and walk through the property.

  7. 6
    Move-in

    You receive keys, a move-in inspection report, and login info for the tenant portal.
