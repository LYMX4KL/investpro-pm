How testing works in v1

  1. Click + Add Property and enter address, monthly rent, beds/baths, etc.
  2. Save — property appears on the public listings page automatically.
  3. Click the property's "Copy share link" to send to a test applicant (or to your team to test).
  4. The applicant opens the link → application form pre-fills the property address + rent + agent (if attributed).
  5. Test applications flow through Accounting → VA → Broker → Term Sheet → Lease → Move-In.
