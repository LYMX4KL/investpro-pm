📋 What you should look at, room-by-room (tips)

Walls: Look for nail holes, scuffs, dents, faded paint.

Floors: Check carpet stains, scratches on hardwood, broken tiles.

Appliances: Open every appliance. Test the burners, dishwasher, garbage disposal.

Plumbing: Run every faucet. Flush every toilet. Check under sinks for leaks.

Smoke/CO detectors: Press the test button on each one.

Windows: Open and close each. Check screens.

Take photos liberally — even of things that look "fine." It's much easier to defend your deposit at move-out with photos than without.
