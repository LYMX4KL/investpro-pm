🔒 Build v0.4-preview · Updated 2026-04-27

📌 What changed in v0.4 (latest)

  • Rental Application form rebuilt to match our actual GLVAR Rev. 11.19 form. Now 13 sections including a new Holding Fee Agreement, full pet acknowledgments, and the 10 GLVAR disclosures.
  • Fees updated to $75 primary applicant / $50 each additional adult (was previously $45 flat).
  • New fields lay groundwork for auto-routing VOR to prior landlords and Employment Verification to current employers (the actual auto-send happens in Phase 3).
  • Reviewer Welcome page (this page!) — so you can find what to look at without hunting.
