Services for Property Owners

You bought the property to make money — not to spend Saturdays unclogging drains. Here's how InvestPro takes care of it for you.

For Owners

One licensed broker. One point of contact.

Many property managers in Las Vegas are part-timers or unlicensed admins. At InvestPro, every account is overseen by a licensed Nevada broker — meaning the person managing your asset is actually accountable to the Real Estate Division.

You get one point of contact, a real-time owner portal, and a clear monthly statement. No nickel-and-diming, no markups on maintenance, no surprise fees.

Nevada-specific lease, NRS 118A security-deposit accounting, Fair Housing compliance, eviction filings handled with our attorney partners. You stay clean, we stay accountable.
